# Scout

**Scout** is an iOS app for couples to manage a shared restaurant and café
wishlist. Instead of a messy notes list, Scout gives you location-aware
sorting, rich info cards, and a collaborative pick feature so you can
always decide where to go — fast.

---

## Tagline
*"Find your next favorite."*

## Visual Identity
- **Colors:** Burnt orange, orange, white
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif, lots of whitespace
- **Motifs:** Compass, map pins, the letter S
- **Icon concepts:**
  - A compass rose with a fork as one of the needles
  - A minimalist map pin shaped like an "S"
  - A scout/binoculars silhouette

---

## The Problem
Couples accumulate restaurant recommendations from friends, social media,
and TikTok videos — but they live in a messy notes app. When it's time
to go out, you don't know which spots are nearby, what to order, or how
to agree on a place without a 20-minute debate.

## The Solution
Scout centralizes your wishlist with real location data, rich info cards,
and a swipe-based pick feature so both people can decide together in
seconds.

---

## Core Features
- Shared wishlist between two accounts
- Location-aware sorting by distance
- Restaurant info cards (hours, dishes, rating, photos)
- Swipe-based "Pick for Us" matching
- Visited tracking with personal notes and ratings
- Food photo/video gallery per restaurant
- TikTok share extension with Gemini-powered caption parsing
- Safari and Apple Maps share extension
- Home screen widget
- Siri Shortcut integration
